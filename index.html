🍝 Aglio e Olio

Classic Italian Pasta with Garlic & Olive Oil

⏱ Prep Time

5 Minutes

🔥 Cook Time

15 Minutes

🍽 Servings

2 People

Ingredients

Spaghetti Garlic Olive Oil Parsley Chili Flakes

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 5 Garlic Cloves (thinly sliced)
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Chili Flakes
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Parsley
  • ½ Cup Reserved Pasta Water
  • Salt & Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Reserve pasta water before draining.
  3. Heat olive oil over low heat.
  4. Sauté garlic until lightly golden.
  5. Add chili flakes and stir.
  6. Add pasta water to create a silky sauce.
  7. Toss spaghetti into the pan.
  8. Add parsley, salt and pepper.
  9. Mix thoroughly and serve immediately!!.

👨‍🍳 Chef's Tips

  • Never burn the garlic.
  • Use premium extra virgin olive oil.
  • Pasta water helps emulsify the sauce.
  • Serve immediately for best texture.
