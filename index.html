Aglio e Olio Pasta

A classic Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. Simple, delicious, and ready in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 4–5 Garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Flakes
  • 2 tbsp Fresh Parsley, chopped
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ cup Reserved Pasta Water
  • Grated Parmesan Cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil salted water in a large pot.
  2. Cook spaghetti until al dente.
  3. Reserve ¼ cup pasta water and drain the pasta.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over low-medium heat.
  5. Add garlic and cook until lightly golden.
  6. Add chili flakes and stir.
  7. Add reserved pasta water and mix.
  8. Toss in the cooked spaghetti.
  9. Add parsley and season with salt.
  10. Serve hot with Parmesan cheese if desired.
  11. Enjoy!!.

Cooking Time

Preparation: 5 minutes

Cooking: 15 minutes
